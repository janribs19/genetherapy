Did You Know?

  • Gene therapy targets the root cause of diseases by correcting genetic mutations.
  • In 1990, the first successful human gene therapy was performed on a 4-year-old girl with a rare immune disorder.
  • Gene therapy has potential applications in treating cancer, genetic disorders, and even heart disease.
  • The global gene therapy market is projected to reach over $10 billion by 2027.
  • CRISPR technology, a groundbreaking gene-editing tool, has revolutionized the field of genetics.
